About

Our School

Imam bashir islamic model academy provide Muslim children with an environment in which they can learn and live Islam. The homogeneity of their culture and values creates social and emotional stability which facilitates and accelerates their learning in general areas of education (math, science, language arts, etc.)1. Islamic schools also develop a strong sense of belonging to the Muslim Ummah1.

Imam bashir islamic model academy has a well-rounded curriculum. The curriculum include the five core subjects of Islam: Quran, Hadith, Fiqh, History, and Arabic¹. Students should learn about the history of Islam and Arabic which is the language of the Quran and Hadith¹. Islamic schools also provide a strong secular education.

Western Curriculum

The school offer a full and balanced curriculum while ensuring the essential of literacy and numeracy are taught effectively. It is also aimed to integrate a solid Islamic standard which will enable the school children to represent their identity practically. Emphasis will be placed on good manners, discipline and respect for Allah’s creation. Subjects as demanded by the Ministry of Education will be covered fully. This will cover area of learning such as:

⦁ Personal, social and emotional development

⦁ Understanding the world

⦁ Communication, language and literacy ( Emphasis will be paid on achieving that pupils who spend at least 3 years in the school are able to understand and converse in Arabic in addition to English language which is the official language. )

⦁ Mathematical development

⦁ Physical development

⦁ Expressive Art and Design development

⦁ Vocational, entrepreneurship and leadership skills. Scheme of work will be made for at least six (6) trending vocations in which a student must attend training in one, a child who has spent three (5) years in the school must have satisfactorily be trained in the chosen vocation.
